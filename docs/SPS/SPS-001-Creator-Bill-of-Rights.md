# SPS-001 — Creator Bill of Rights

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0

Creators have the right to:

1. Own their data.
2. Privacy by default.
3. Transparency.
4. Choice of platform, provider, and workflow.
5. Use StreamPilot without AI.
6. Leave and export their data.
7. Security.
8. Understand configuration and permissions.
9. Accessibility.
10. Community voice.
11. A complete core experience without subscription.
12. Creative control.
13. Work offline whenever practical.

## Promise

Every decision made by the StreamPilot project should strengthen these rights.
