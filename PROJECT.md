# The StreamPilot Project

## Purpose

This document introduces StreamPilot and explains how its guiding principles, documentation, and development process fit together.

Before contributing code, contributors should understand the project's philosophy and technical foundation.

## What is StreamPilot?

StreamPilot is an open-source Creator Intelligence Platform.

Its purpose is to help creators understand, improve, and grow through thoughtful analytics, actionable insights, and creator-first tooling.

StreamPilot supports creators without replacing their creativity or ownership.

## Mission

Help creators improve every stream through thoughtful intelligence, reliable tools, and creator-first design.

## Vision

Become the most trusted open-source Creator Intelligence Platform while respecting creator ownership, privacy, and creativity.

## Core Values

- Creator First
- Local First
- AI is Optional
- Security by Design
- Open Source
- Transparency
- Reliability
- Simplicity
- Community Driven
- Never Stream Alone

## Decision Making

When making engineering decisions, ask:

1. Does this help creators?
2. Does it align with the Manifesto?
3. Is it the simplest reliable solution?
4. Is AI truly necessary?
5. Does it preserve creator ownership?
6. Will contributors understand this in two years?

## North Star

Every stream should make the next stream better.
